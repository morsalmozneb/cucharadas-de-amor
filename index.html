Why It Matters

In Mexico's public school system, children who are not enrolled on time can permanently lose their spot. In communities like Lomas de la Primavera, where extreme poverty makes timely enrollment nearly impossible, this means many children never attend school at all.

Without elementary school, there is no path to middle school, high school, or formal employment. The result is generational: adults in this community who missed their chance as children cannot read or write today. Their own children now face the same impossible odds.

Cucharadas de Amor breaks this cycle by giving children the education, protection, and support they need to build a different future.

What We Do

Education

Academic support and schooling for children ages 7–13 who are behind or without access to school.

Personal Development

Workshops on values, self-esteem, and parenting skills for the whole family.

Nutritional Support

Daily meals for students and subsidized food baskets for families.

Our Philosophy

The most powerful thing we give isn't food. It's love.

We've learned something after almost 30 years: What truly changes a child's life isn't only food, or education, or resources. It's knowing that someone loves them. That someone shows up. That they are worthy.

We've watched children walk through our doors carrying that weight and graduate standing in their own light.

— Cucharadas de Amor A.C.
