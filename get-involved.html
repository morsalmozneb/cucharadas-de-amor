Volunteering

Make a Difference Through Service

Volunteering at Cucharadas de Amor is a meaningful opportunity to be part of a community effort that transforms lives through service, empathy, and social commitment. Every volunteer brings their time, talent, and heart to create a supportive learning environment for children and families.

We believe that everyone can contribute through their unique skills, creativity, and willingness to help.

Types of Volunteering

Daily Activities

Support the center's daily operations: accompany children, assist with educational activities, organize materials, prepare spaces, and help with workshops.

Special Events

Help plan and run community events, celebrations, campaigns, and recreational activities that strengthen community bonds.

Remote Volunteering

Contribute from home by creating materials, providing creative support, design help, or organizational development assistance.

Who Can Volunteer

There is no strict minimum age. Children and teens are welcome when accompanied by a responsible adult. School groups can participate with teacher supervision. This makes volunteering a formative experience for younger generations, fostering values like solidarity, responsibility, and social commitment.

Donate Resources

We accept donations of non-perishable foods, basic household products, cleaning supplies, and personal hygiene items. These contribute directly to the food baskets we distribute to families.
